Agriculture in India: Information About Indian Agriculture & Its Importance

In India, agriculture is the primary source of livelihood for ~55% of the population.

Agriculture and Allied Industries
ADVANTAGE INDIA

Robust Demand

Factors contributing to the sharp growth in demand for processed foods include increasing urbanization, rising disposable incomes, changing spending patterns, and the growing need for convenience foods in dual-income nuclear families.

Attractive Opportunities

India's seaweed farming sector, currently valued at Rs. 200 crore, is projected to grow significantly over the next decade, benefiting 1.6 million people. The alcoholic beverages industry also holds strong potential globally.

Policy Support

Online, competitive, transparent bidding system with over 1.74 crore farmers under the National Agriculture Market (e-NAM) scheme. New PM Matsya Sampada Yojana with major investment to strengthen fisheries.

Competitive Advantage

India’s diverse agro-climatic conditions and vast natural resources provide strong advantages in food processing and raw material availability for agriculture-related industries.
